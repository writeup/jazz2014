The Artistic Director's Guide to Jazz - Live! with Margaret Stowe

At each session of The Artistic Director's Guide to Jazz - Live!, Artistic Director Josh Grossman chats with a local musician about, and plays tracks from, some of the artists performing at the 2014 TD Toronto Jazz Festival. For this session, Josh sat down with exciting local guitarist Margaret Stowe to discuss some of the guitarists appearing at this year’s festival.

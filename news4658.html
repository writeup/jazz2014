More AD's Guide to Jazz - Live! now online

The final two Artistic Director's Guide to Jazz - Live! sessions are now online. At each session, Artistic Director Josh Grossman chats with a special guest about a different series of concerts at this year's TD Toronto Jazz Festival. This year's sessions were recorded by Regent Radio. All four sessions are now available online as audio podcasts: Josh chats with Alex Samaras about vocalists; with Larnell Lewis about the influence of R&B; with Bill King about playing solo; and with Margaret Stowe about guitarists.

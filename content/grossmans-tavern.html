Grossman's Tavern

379 Spadina Avenue
M5T 2G3 Toronto, ON
Canada
See map: Google Maps
416-977-7000
Website: 
http://www.grossmanstavern.com

Open since 1943, Grossman’s Tavern is one of the city’s longest-running live music venues, and Toronto’s self-described “Home of the Blues.” Rock, folk, roots and jazz acts are also at home here.

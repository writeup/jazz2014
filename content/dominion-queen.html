Dominion On Queen

500 Queen Street East
M5A 1T7 Toronto, ON
Canada
See map: Google Maps
416-368-6893
Website: 
http://www.dominiononqueen.com

The Dominion presents many of the finest jazz and blues musicians weekly. From Wednesday through Sunday, come for great performances as well as fine food and drink. From intimate trios to big bands, the Dominion offers the full spectrum of jazz styles. Whether Latin, funk, acid or classic standards, you are sure to enjoy them all. Enjoy over 16 regional and craft beers on tap, imported brews and wonderful wines. Have a snack or a full meal.

Wheelchair accessible

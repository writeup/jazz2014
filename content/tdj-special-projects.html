TDJ Special Projects - Online Submission

Use this form to make an electronic submission to the TDJ Special Projects initiative. Items marked with an asterisk (*) are required; bio and project description must either be pasted into the space provided OR uploaded.

For complete information on the TDJ Special Projects visit torontojazz.com/special.

Completed application
Attach your completed TDJ Special Projects application form here, either as a doc or pdf file. Please ensure that all of the fields on pages 5, 7 and 8 of the application have been completed.
Files must be less than 800 KB.
Allowed file types: pdf doc.
Bio
Please paste here, or upload below as a PDF, the following: One paragraph of biographical information for each artist involved in your proposed project If applicable, one paragraph of biographical information for the ensemble involved in your proposed project (this is in addition to the biographical information the each artist) If the ensemble is being formed specifically for the proposed project (i.e. it has not existed in the past), one paragraph explaining why this particular group of artists is coming together Biographical information should, as much as possible, be relevant to and provide context for the proposed project. Maximum upload size: 500 Kb
Files must be less than 1000 KB.
Allowed file types: pdf.
Project description
Please paste here, or upload as a PDF below, a detailed description of your proposed project, including the following information: The nature of your proposed project, including a synopsis of the form and content Your artistic goals and objectives An explanation detailing how this project falls outside of your usual activities The market and audience you wish to reach with the proposed project If you are proposing a free event, you must justify why an admission fee is not being charged Any other information that you feel is relevant to the description of the proposed project Maximum upload size: 1 MB; the project description should be a maximum of two pages.
Files must be less than 1.46 MB.
Allowed file types: pdf.
Audio/Video support materials
Upload your audio/visual samples below in mp3, avi or mov format. Remember: If your Special Project is performance-based, please submit three recent audio samples of your work. Samples should be from the last three years and should, as is possible, relate to the Special Project. Unless your performance includes a specific visual component, please do not send video samples of your work. If your project involves a special guest, send two samples of your work, and one sample of your guest artist’s work. If your Special Project is a workshop or is educational in nature, please submit two recent video samples of your work. If your project involves a special guest, send one sample of your work, and one sample of your guest artist’s work. Maximum upload size: 10 Mb per sample.
Files must be less than 14.65 MB.
Allowed file types: avi mov mp3.
Files must be less than 14.65 MB.
Allowed file types: avi mov mp3.
Files must be less than 14.65 MB.
Allowed file types: mp3.

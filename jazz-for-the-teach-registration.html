Jazz for the Teach Registration

The 2014 edition of Jazz for the Teach features two interactive sessions geared towards middle and high school music teachers looking to hone their jazz teaching skills. Jazz for the Teach takes place on Sunday, June 22 at The Paintbox Bistro, 555 Dundas Street East in Regent Park. From 12-2 pm, Andrew Scott and Lisa Martinelly will lead a session on jazz fundamentals and small group playing; from 3-5 pm Paul Read will lead a session on big bands. You are welcome to attend one or both sessions.

Participation in Jazz for the Teach is FREE but registration is required. Please complete this registration form by June 13th.
